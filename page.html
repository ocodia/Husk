Sub-topic 2

You may pay less council tax if someone living in your property has a permanent and substantial disability. To qualify for disabled band reduction, the property must have at least one of the following:

  • been adapted to allow wheelchair use indoors
  • a second bathroom, shower room or kitchen used to meet the needs of the person with the disability
  • a room used solely to meet the needs of the person with the disability
